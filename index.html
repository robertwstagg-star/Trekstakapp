Frequently Asked Questions

? How do I access my free cities?

When you first sign in, you choose 3 cities to explore for free. All walking tours, neighborhoods, and travel tools are available for those cities. To unlock all 115 cities, upgrade to Trekker or Voyager.

? How do I restore my subscription?

Open TrekStak, go to Profile, then Settings. Scroll down to Subscription and tap "Restore Purchases." Your subscription will be restored if purchased with the same Apple ID.

? Does TrekStak work offline?

Yes! All city guides, walking tours, neighborhoods, and food trails are stored on your device. You can browse and navigate without an internet connection. Only photos require connectivity.

? How do I cancel my subscription?

Open your iPhone Settings, tap your name at the top, then Subscriptions, find TrekStak, and tap Cancel Subscription. You keep access until the end of your current billing period.

? How does Trek Tracker work?

Trek Tracker reads your step count and walking distance from Apple Health automatically. Make sure you grant TrekStak permission in Settings, then Privacy & Security, then Health.

? Can I use TrekStak on multiple devices?

Yes! Sign in with the same Apple ID on all your devices. Your subscription, favorites, and walking progress sync automatically via iCloud.
